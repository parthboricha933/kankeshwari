 Our Story

Where Every Meal Feels Like
a Seaside Getaway

Nestled in the heart of the beautiful Union Territory of Diu, Sea Cafe & Food Destination is more than a restaurant — it's a culinary landmark. Born from a passion for fresh ingredients and warm hospitality, we have been welcoming families, tourists, and food lovers from across India and beyond.

From our freshly-caught seafood specials to fragrant Indian curries, crispy fast food bites, and refreshing beverages — every plate tells a story of love, tradition, and coastal flavour. Our relaxed beachside ambiance, hygienic open kitchen, and attentive service make us the go-to dining destination in Diu.

 Our Promise

Why Guests Love Sea Cafe

Every visit is crafted to delight — from the first sip to the last bite.

Fresh Ingredients

We source only the freshest produce, straight from local farms and daily seafood markets to ensure every dish is vibrant and nutritious.

Hygienic Kitchen

Our open-concept kitchen follows the highest hygiene standards. Cleanliness is our culture — from the prep table to your plate.

Beachside Ambience

Dine with the sound of waves and the scent of the sea breeze. Our outdoor and semi-alfresco seating creates a truly magical atmosphere.

Family Friendly

A welcoming space for everyone — from toddlers to grandparents. Our menu, seating, and staff are all designed with families in mind.

Fast Service

We respect your time. Our trained team ensures prompt, cheerful service so your dining experience is seamless and enjoyable.

Quality Food

From the spice blend to the garnish, every element is carefully considered. We never compromise on taste, quality, or presentation.
